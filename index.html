How to Make a Podcast

Assembly time: 60 minutes-7 days
Awesome rating: 100% awesome

It is easier than ever to get your voice heard thanks to modern technology and communication methods. One of the best ways to get your words out is by making a podcast! In this tutorial, we will look at a step by step method to create ideas and make a podcast. Then we'll look at how to share it with the world.

  • Things You'll Need

  • Pen and paper or other notetaking device
  • Microphone(s) or other recording device
  • Iphone or Camera(s)
  • Computer
  • An audio software like Adobe Audition (Paid) or Audacity (Free)
  • Friends (Optional but recommended)
  • Water or tea (To stay hydrated)

  1. Write down ideas for your podcast.

    This is the first and arguable most important step when making your podcast. Sit down and ask yourself the following questions.

    • What do I want to say?
    • Who is my audience?
    • How many hosts do I want on the podcast and who are they?
    • Who do I want to talk to on my podcast?
    • How do I want to tell my story?
    • How long do I want the podcast to be?

  2. Create a script.

    Once you have answered your questions above, sit down with your co-hosts (if you have them) and start to put together a script for your podcast.

  3. Gather your recording materials.

    Get your equipment and put together your recording setup. Once you have your setup, you can put it in a designated recording spot so you don't have to setup and tear down the space every time you want to record.

  4. Sit down and record the podcast.

    Get your co-hosts and guest speakers (if you have them) and start recording! Spend as much or as little time as you would like to recording your podcast and have fun with it!

    Have fun with your podcast recording session!

  5. Edit your podcast.

    Once you have all of your audio recorded, edit the footage in your audio software and export it as a .wav file.

  6. Upload your podcast.

    Upload your content to the podcast platform of your choice. Popular options are Spotify, Apple Podcasts, Soundcloud, and Google Podcasts.

  7. Promote your podcast.

    Promote your podcast on social media to spread awareness! This can be through Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, or another social media platform.

    Choose your favorite social media platform!
